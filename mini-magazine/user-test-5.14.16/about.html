    Space is an online lifestyle magazine

    With a focus on small spaces & minimalism, Space is divided into chapters comprising of stories on places & people paired with striking photography. Included in every volume is a review or guide featuring style & culture.

    The owners of anything featured in Space are always referred to in their own feature page. The items in the moodboard gallery are found items on the internet and are not my own. If you have a problem with your work appearing here, please let me know & it will be promptly removed. Thank you for visiting.
