Creative Brief

Lara Dominguez
March 12, 2016

  1. Project Title: Space

  2. Description: A lifestyle magazine focused on small spaces.

  3. Audience:
    • urban readers with a city lifestyle
    • creatives looking for new ideas for their living and work spaces
    • young working-class
    • minimalists

  4. Mission: To present and inform readers of
    • small and creative living spaces,
    • design and lifestyle ideas for a minimalist lifestyle - living more with less,
    • reviews of small-scale restaurants,
    • and product highlights.
